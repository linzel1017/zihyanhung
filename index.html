I’m Rachel, a comic artist from Taiwan.
I create romance comics and illustrations with
90’s/80’s and sci-fi elements.

I studied film making in college and learned
how to tell stories through different media.

I enjoy reading suspense novels, singing karaoke,
and exploring psychology for my creations.
I also like to experiment with different creative formats,
such as playable webcomics.

If you have work opportunities for me,
please contact me at:
